How it works

Forward a reel. Get a vault.

Four steps, under 30 seconds from DM to dissected. No timeline scrubbing, no Shazam tabs, no transcription copy-paste.

— 01
Forward or paste the link
DM the Reel to @reelkit on Instagram, or drop the URL into the dashboard. TikTok and YouTube Shorts land next month.
— 02
We separate the four tracks
Our models isolate the video, the BGM (matched against a commercial library), the SFX stingers, and the creator's speech — each as a standalone, scrubbable file.
— 03
Auto-tag, file, and mood-board
Every asset lands tagged by mood, tempo, scene, and voice energy. Drop them into mood boards you build per video, per client, or per vibe.
— 04
Export straight into your NLE
One-click send to CapCut, Premiere, Resolve, or Final Cut — clips arrive pre-synced with metadata intact, ready to drop on a timeline.
Inside the vault

Every asset tagged, previewed, exported.

Not a folder of MP3s. A searchable, mood-boarded, NLE-ready library of every creative asset you've ever vibed with.

01 · Personal vault

A searchable library of every reel you've ever vibed with.

Search "golden-hour slow-pan with a melancholy piano loop" and get every matching asset across every reel you've ever forwarded. Filter by mood, tempo, duration, voice energy, or the creator you clipped it from.

mood: nostalgic tempo: 92 bpm voice: low-energy
02 · Mood boards

Build a board per video. Or per feeling.

Drag tracks into named boards — "March launch hero", "Lo-fi recap energy", "That one specific Daniel Schiffer feel". Boards are shareable with collaborators.

03 · Isolated preview

Scrub each track alone.

Mute the voice, hear just the BGM. Solo the SFX. Finally understand what's actually making the reel feel the way it does.

04 · One-click export

Send to your editor.

Direct send to CapCut, Premiere, Resolve, Final Cut. Metadata, tempo, and tags preserved on the way in.
